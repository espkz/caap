WHAT IS CAAP?

In the realm of academia, the comprehension of research papers poses a challenge to students and researchers alike due to technical jargon and complex concepts. To address this, we created Capture Assistant in Academic Papers (CAAP), a tool designed to facilitate the understanding of academic papers through keyphrase extraction and contextual explanations.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

It extracts key phrases and explains them in simple terms. By scanning papers that are in a PDF format, it highlights important points to make research more accessible.
